The cells reorganize themselves and reconstruct a new DNA code containing 58 chromosomes, until they begin to assemble a new type of very primitive and watery organism.

We then extracted living cells from a person in the laboratory and put them in contact with these cells. The results are final. Living human cells gradually transform until they take on the appearance of a Z cell.

The entity is therefore a physical threat, but also spreads like a virus, it can cause the death very quickly of men in contact with it, by reprogramming their DNA.
