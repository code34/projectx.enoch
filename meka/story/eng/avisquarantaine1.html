While searching the rubble of this desolate village, you find a medical note. This note was distributed, and plastered on the walls so that the population can read it.

Public notice - quarantine

The government promulgates a state of emergency and quarantine. Do not leave your home until further notice.

It has been proven that a highly contagious virus is spreading rapidly. The virus is spread from person to person through close and direct physical contact with infected body fluids, the most infectious of which are blood, stool and vomit. Accordingly, you should stay away from anyone who is infected, and immediately report cases to us at this telephone number: 212 521.
