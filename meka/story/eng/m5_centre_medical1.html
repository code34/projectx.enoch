The medical center is a small establishment with around fifty ultra-specialized beds. The building has remained in good condition although there is an unnamed mess. Several cupboards, drawers are half open. The equipment is ultra hitech. This building is undoubtedly part of the elite care units of the country.

It suits you, as there is a bit of useful material to collect, bandages, alcohol, medication.

At the back of the building, you enter a confined room which it seems has benefited from extraordinary importance and resources. The appearance of this room resembles a mortuary and several glazed boxes in which experiments have been carried out are next to each other. You realize that something unusual has happened here.
