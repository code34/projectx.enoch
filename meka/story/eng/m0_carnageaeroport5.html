Intercommunications between the tanks allow the group to be filled more quickly. For this, the intercommunications are equipped with a non-return valve. A small non-return valve located at the bottom of the tanks between the manifold and the other tank, makes it possible to compensate for a damage to the transfer pump. A large non-return valve located halfway between the two reservoirs allows rapid filling by gravity when filling.
