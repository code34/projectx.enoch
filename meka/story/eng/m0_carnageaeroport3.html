The gearbox is an essential element of the helicopter. It allows the transmission of power from the motors to the main rotor, as well as to the anti-torque rotor.

Where to find a gearbox gear

Several possibilities are available to you to find this equipment. Although mechanically not very complex, it is not possible to shape this object yourself. It will therefore be necessary to continue a search across the territory in military, industrial buildings, or even garages to find this part.

Your knowledge of mechanics allows you to have a precise idea of the part to find. So there will be no doubts possible once you see it.
